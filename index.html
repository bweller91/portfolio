Our expert technicians provide high-precision soldering services for a wide range of electronic components. Perfect for restoring functionality to vintage electronics, damaged devices, and custom automotive modules.

Wiring Repairs

Specializing in complex wiring faults, we offer comprehensive repair solutions. From identifying and patching frayed wires to complete harness overhauls, we fix electrical issues in vehicles, homes, and industrial equipment. Our meticulous approach guarantees optimal signal integrity and power distribution, preventing future failures.

Vehicle Coding

Unlock hidden features and customize your vehicle's electronic control modules. Our vehicle coding services allow for personalized settings like enabling/disabling warning chimes, activating hidden light configurations, and reconfiguring onboard systems. Enhance your driving experience and tailor your car to your exact preferences.

Gearbox TCU Programming

Optimize your vehicle's transmission performance with our Transmission Control Unit (TCU) programming. Whether you're looking for smoother shifts, faster gear changes, or custom shift points, we calibrate the TCU to deliver a more responsive and engaging driving dynamic. Also useful for adapting newer transmissions to older chassis.

Vehicle Servicing + Parts

Keep your car running at its best with our comprehensive vehicle servicing. We perform routine maintenance, including oil changes, brake inspections, filter replacements, and fluid top-ups. We only use high-quality, genuine parts to ensure optimal performance and longevity for your vehicle. Thorough checks and preventative maintenance.

Vehicle Diagnostics

Identify underlying vehicle issues quickly and accurately with our advanced diagnostic services. Utilizing state-of-the-art diagnostic tools, we scan your car's electronic systems to pinpoint fault codes and performance irregularities. Get a clear understanding of your vehicle's health and make informed decisions about repairs.

Vehicle ECU Programming

Transform your vehicle's performance with Engine Control Unit (ECU) remapping. Our ECU programming optimizes engine parameters like fuel ignition timing, boost pressure, and rev limits. Choose from different modes: **Eco** for improved fuel efficiency, **Mixed** for a balance of power and economy, or **High Performance** for maximum horsepower and torque gains.
